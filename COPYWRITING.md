## 7. FAQ

**Q1: Is it really free?**
**A1:** Yes. The core tone generator is completely free — all waveforms, full frequency range, visualization, and mobile support. No signup needed. Pro adds advanced features for power users.

**Q2: Do I need to create an account?**
**A2:** No. The free tool works instantly in your browser. We don't collect your email or personal data for basic use.

**Q3: How accurate are the frequencies?**
**A3:** Frequencies are generated digitally via the Web Audio API with sample-accurate timing. For most testing purposes — headphones, speakers, instrument tuning — the precision is more than sufficient. This is not a calibrated laboratory instrument.

**Q4: Can I use this for medical diagnosis?**
**A4:** No. Tone Generator is not a medical device. It can help you identify a tinnitus frequency to discuss with your doctor, but it cannot diagnose hearing loss or any medical condition. Always consult a healthcare professional.

**Q5: Is my audio data stored?**
**A5:** No. All audio generation happens locally in your browser. We never upload, store, or process your audio. Your settings are optionally saved in your browser's localStorage only.

**Q6: What happens if I cancel Pro?**
**A6:** You can cancel anytime through the Creem Customer Portal. You'll keep Pro access until the end of your billing period, then revert to the free version. Your presets won't be accessible until you resubscribe.

**Q7: How is this different from onlinetonegenerator.com?**
**A7:** Three things: cleaner design (no cluttered interface), better mobile experience, and no intrusive ads. We focus on doing one thing well — generating precise tones instantly.

**Q8: Can I export the audio?**
**A8:** WAV export is available in Pro. Free users can record their browser output using system tools or upgrade to Pro for one-click export.

TECHNICAL FAQ

WHICH SAMPLE RATE IS USED? expand_more
The generator utilizes the Web Audio API running at your hardware's native sample rate (typically 44.1kHz or 48kHz) to ensure maximum fidelity.
ARE THERE ALIASING ISSUES? expand_more
We use band-limited oscillators for non-sine waveforms to prevent aliasing artifacts in the higher frequency ranges.

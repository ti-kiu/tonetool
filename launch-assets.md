## 长描述（150 words）
Tone Generator is a free web-based audio tool that lets you generate precise frequencies from 1Hz to 20kHz. It includes 11 specialized tools: tone generator, frequency sweep, headphone test, hearing test, tinnitus matcher, instrument tuner, subwoofer test, binaural beats, white noise, 440Hz and 432Hz tones.

All tools run directly in your browser using Web Audio API — no downloads, no signups, no ads. Works on mobile, tablet, and desktop. You can even download WAV files of any tone you create.

Whether you're testing new headphones, tuning your guitar, matching tinnitus sounds for your doctor, or checking your subwoofer response, Tone Generator has you covered.
